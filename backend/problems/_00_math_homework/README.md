# Math Homework

## Part One

You (the story’s main protagonist) are looking forward to the ACM General Event hosted on Friday, March 20th. Unfortunately, you still have one math assignment due the same day, and you haven’t even started it yet (classic move).

Luckily, you’re an experienced programmer, so you decide to leverage your problem-solving and coding skills to tackle the homework with ease.

You are given a series of integers, one per line (your puzzle input). Your task is to compute the sum of all the numbers.

For example, given the following input:

```
24
53
109
67
80
12
8
```

The sum of these numbers is: `24 + 53 + 109 + 67 + 80 + 12 + 8 = 353`. So, the answer for this example would be **353**.

Your actual input will be much larger. Following these instructions, **what is the final answer to your homework assignment?**

## Part Two

Oops! It turns out you misread the assignment instructions. Instead of simply summing all the numbers, you are required to process them differently (of course it wouldn’t be that easy).

This time, you must:
- Compute the sum of all **odd** numbers.
- Compute the sum of all **even** numbers.
- Multiply these two sums together to get the final answer.

Using the same example as before:
- The sum of the odd numbers is `53 + 109 + 67 = 229`
- The sum of the even numbers is `24 + 80 + 12 + 8 = 124`

Multiplying these results gives: `229 × 124 = 28396`

Now that you know the correct instructions, **what is the final answer to your homework assignment?**
